Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®

Getting started with your Trezor wallet has never been easier. This guide will walk you through the setup process for Trezor.io/start (Version 22.9.3) and ensure your crypto security is top-notch. For more details, visit our website.

Steps to Setup Your Trezor Device

  1. Unbox your Trezor device and connect it to your computer.
  2. Navigate to Trezor.io/start to begin the setup process.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to configure your device.
  4. Ensure your device is securely stored and backed up.
  5. Explore the features of your new Trezor wallet.
