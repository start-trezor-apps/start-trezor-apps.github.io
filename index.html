Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®

Welcome to Trezor.io/Start®, your ultimate guide for getting started with the Trezor hardware wallet (Version 22.9.3). Setting up your Trezor wallet is crucial for ensuring the highest level of security for your crypto assets. Follow our step-by-step instructions to get your Trezor device up and running smoothly.

Setup Instructions

To start, connect your Trezor device to your computer and visit Trezor.io/start. Follow the on-screen instructions to configure your hardware wallet. The process is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring that even first-time users can easily set up their Trezor wallet.

For a detailed walkthrough, visit our setup guide or refer to the official Trezor.io/start page.

FAQs

How do I reset my Trezor device?

To reset your Trezor device, follow the instructions on the Trezor.io/start page. You will need to access the device settings and select the option to reset.

Can I use Trezor with multiple devices?

Yes, Trezor can be used with multiple devices. Ensure that you follow the setup instructions on Trezor.io/start to configure each device correctly.

What should I do if I lose my Trezor device?

If you lose your Trezor device, you can recover your assets using your recovery seed. Refer to the recovery instructions on Trezor.io/start for more details.

How do I update my Trezor firmware?

To update your Trezor firmware, connect your device to your computer and visit Trezor.io/start. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the update.

Is Trezor compatible with all cryptocurrencies?

Trezor supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies. For the full list of supported assets, visit the official Trezor.io/start page.
